Seltos Verdict

The Seltos is Kia’s first product in India, and it has a lot going for itself. It packs a striking design, is loaded with features to the brim and offers top-notch interior quality in the segment. ... Sure, Kia Motors could have priced it aggressively; however, you are spoilt for choice with the many variants on offer and the engine/transmission combinations.
